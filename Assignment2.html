Explanation:

The robot will take a few seconds to load, to give time for the images to load.

When the 'On/Off' button is pressed, the robots eyes will light up to signify it is 'On'. When pressed again, the eyes will turn off to signify 'Off'.

When click any part of the canvas, lasers will shoot from the robots eyes to the location clicked. When a new task is done, the laser is cleared off the canvas.

However, if the far right or left side of the canvas is clicked, the robot will shift in that direction.

If the right or left arm is clicked, that arm will spin for 2 seconds.

After 3 tasks have been done, the robot turns itself off.

When the 'Charge' button is pressed, you must wait a few seconds until the robot charges fully. The robot will charge if at least 1 task has been done.

When the window is resized, the canvas adjusts so it is visible on the screen and the coordinates stay relative. Therefore it is mobile and desktop responsive.
